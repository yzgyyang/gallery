About me

This is Guangyuan Yang, a hobbyist photographer grew up in Yangzhou, China and currently lives in the Kitchener-Waterloo Region for undergraduate studies. I did a lot of landscape, street and travel photos. Sometimes I also shoot portraits for family and friends and cover local events. I am an Executive at the University of Waterloo Photography Club since 2016.

Please feel free to message me for shoots, high-res photos, or basically anything!
